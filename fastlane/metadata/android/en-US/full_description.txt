FCL Finance is a comprehensive financial management application designed specifically for faith community management.

Features:

• Track Weekly Collections: Record tithes, offerings, and other weekly giving
• Expense Management: Log and categorize church expenses
• Activity Logs: Maintain detailed records of all financial transactions
• Member Management: Track member information and contributions
• Reporting: Generate financial reports for analysis and planning
• Secure Data: Local database with optional backup functionality
• Offline Support: Work offline and sync when connected

The application provides a user-friendly interface for managing church finances, with support for multiple collection categories and expense tracking. All data is stored locally on your device for privacy and security.

FCL Finance helps faith communities maintain accurate financial records and make informed decisions about resource allocation.
